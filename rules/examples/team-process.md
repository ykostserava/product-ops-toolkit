# Team Process (Hard Rules)

Example of an **unconditional rule**: short, absolute constraints that stop Claude
from assuming resources or ceremonies your team doesn't have. Edit to match yours.

- **Kanban workflow ONLY** — never mention sprints, sprint planning, velocity, or
  sprint-based elements in stories, plans, or reports
- **No dedicated designers** — don't assume design resources exist; design tasks go
  to the engineers who own the surface
- Confirm organizational resources/constraints before assuming they exist
